TRIVANDRUM – KERALA TRAVEL GUIDE

Trivandrum or Thiruvananthapuram is the capital city of Kerala. Located on the western coastal regions of India, this metropolis is also the IT hub of God’s Own Country and one of the most sought after tourist destinations in Kerala.

Once cited as the ‘Evergreen City of India’ by Mahatma Gandhi, Trivandrum has a diverse history and is a home to some of the most colourful and striking cultures. During the ancient times, the city had served as a major spice trading point among several Asian countries.

The city is built across seven of the most captivating hills and is dotted with several pious temples and religious sites. Though the city witness a number of rulers in its past, it has endured its beauty and liveliness in the most splendid ways, and has become of the highest visited tourist destinations in Kerala.

Most Popular Tourist Destinations in Trivandrum

Napier Museum

If you want to revisit the history of Kerala, you must visit the Napier Museum in Trivandrum. One of the oldest museums in the entire state, is has a large collection of bronze artefacts, ivory carvings, ancient musical instruments, and other traditional items.

Neyyar Wildlife Sanctuary

For the wildlife lovers to Trivandrum, they must visit the Neyyar Wildlife Sanctuary which is around 35km from the heart of the city. A natural abode to exotic wildlife, it is known for its significant Lion, Elephant, Tiger, Leopard, Nilgiri Tahr, and several other endangered species.

Varkala Beach

One of the virgin beaches in Kerala, Varkala is an ultimate destination to unwind and rejuvenate while enjoying the prismatic beauty of the Arabian Sea. Compared to other popular beaches in Kerala, Varkala Beach is less crowded and makes it for a serene beach experience.
